Making tea

  1. Fill a kettle with water
  2. Put the kettle on the stove top
  3. Turn on the flame
  4. Wait until the water is boiling
  5. Gather herbs
  6. Put herbs in a tea strainer
  7. Put the tea strainer in an empty mug
  8. Pour boiling water into the mug
  9. Let it steep for a few minutes & cool
